📞 Emergency Action Steps

If You're in Immediate Danger:

  1. Call Emergency Services: 10177 (South Africa) or local emergency number
  2. Activate SafetyHelp: Press emergency button or scan QR code
  3. Move to Safety: Get to a secure location if possible
  4. Notify Network: LifeSync automatically alerts your emergency contacts
  5. Follow Instructions: Emergency responders will guide you through next steps

If Someone You Know is in Danger:

  • Check their LifeSync status and location
  • Contact emergency services with their details
  • Coordinate with other guardians in their network
  • Follow emergency response protocols
