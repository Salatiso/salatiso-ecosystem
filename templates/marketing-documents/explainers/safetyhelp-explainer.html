What is SafetyHelp?

SafetyHelp is LifeSync's comprehensive safety coordination system that uses dynamic QR codes to create instant safety networks. Unlike static QR codes, SafetyHelp codes are intelligent, context-aware, and work completely offline using Bluetooth mesh networking.
