📋 Phase-by-Phase Guide

Phase 1: Account Creation

Welcome & Account Setup

Get started with a secure account that protects your privacy while enabling meaningful connections.

  • Email verification and secure password setup
  • Basic profile information collection
  • Privacy policy review and consent
  • Language preference selection
  • Initial security settings configuration
Phase 2: Trust Foundation

Building Your Trust Profile

Create a comprehensive profile that establishes credibility and helps you find compatible connections.

  • Personal background and values assessment
  • Relationship goals and preferences
  • Compatibility indicators and deal-breakers
  • Trust level preferences and boundaries
  • Profile verification options
Phase 3: Safety Configuration

SafetyHelp Setup

Establish your personal safety network with QR codes, emergency contacts, and offline capabilities.

  • SafetyHelp QR code generation
  • Emergency contact configuration
  • Follow Me Home feature setup
  • Incident reporting preferences
  • Offline safety protocols
Phase 4: Relationship Context

Context-Specific Configuration

Configure settings for romance, business, community, and family relationships.

  • Relationship context prioritization
  • Context-specific privacy settings
  • Compatibility matching preferences
  • Communication style preferences
  • Relationship boundary settings
Phase 5: Launch & Connect

Go Live & Start Connecting

Complete final checks and begin your journey with confidence and safety.

  • Profile review and final adjustments
  • Initial compatibility matching
  • Dashboard familiarization
  • First connection guidance
  • Ongoing support resources
