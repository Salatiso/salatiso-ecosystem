The Life CV

The Life CV is a dynamic, holistic profile that captures more than just your job history. It's a living document that showcases your full range of capabilities, ensuring you get recognized for all your talents.

  • Verifiable Skills: Log competencies from formal courses, on-the-job training, and real-world application.
  • Project Portfolio: Showcase tangible work you've completed with descriptions, links, and images.
  • Community Contributions: Get recognition for mentorship, volunteering, and other vital, often overlooked, roles.
  • Life Experience: Turn informal learning and life lessons into valuable, recognized experience points.

Plan & Develop

Proactive career management is key to long-term success. Our planning tools help you set a course, identify opportunities, and stay on track to achieve your ambitions.

  • Career Path Mapper: Visualize potential career trajectories and the skills needed to get there.
  • Personal Development Plan: Create a structured, actionable plan to acquire new skills and achieve your goals.
  • Skills Audit: Identify your strengths and areas for development with our guided questionnaires.
  • Mentorship Agreements: Formalize mentorship relationships to accelerate your growth and knowledge sharing.

Practical Tools

Access a comprehensive library of templates and forms for every stage of your career lifecycle, ensuring you are always prepared and professional.

  • Professional CV Templates: Choose from multiple styles to create a polished, professional resume.
  • Cover Letter Builders: Craft compelling cover letters tailored to specific job applications.
  • Resignation Letters: Handle your departure professionally with our clear and concise templates.
  • Employee Grievance Forms: Ensure your concerns are documented and addressed formally and fairly.
