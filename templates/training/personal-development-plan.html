Personal Development Plan

(PDP)

1. Purpose of this Plan

A Personal Development Plan (PDP) is a proactive tool for employees to plan their own learning and professional growth in collaboration with their manager. It helps to identify an individual's career aspirations and the skills and knowledge needed to achieve them, creating a clear roadmap for development that benefits both the employee and the organization.

2. How to Use

This PDP should be a living document, reviewed regularly during one-on-one meetings. The employee should take the lead in identifying their development goals, while the manager provides guidance, support, and helps to identify relevant resources and opportunities. This plan should align with the feedback from performance reviews and the company's broader training objectives.
