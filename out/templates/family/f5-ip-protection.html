🔬 Patent Protection for Your Inventions

Patents protect new, useful inventions and give you exclusive rights for up to 20 years

1. Invention Assessment

Determine if your idea is patentable: new, useful, and non-obvious

2. Prior Art Search

Research existing patents and publications to ensure novelty

3. Patent Drafting

Write detailed patent specification with claims, drawings, and description

4. Filing Application

Submit patent application to CIPC with required forms and fees

5. Examination

CIPC examines application and may request amendments or clarifications

6. Grant & Maintenance

Patent granted if approved; annual renewal fees required to maintain protection

Patent Costs in South Africa

  • Filing fee: R200-R500
  • Examination fee: R1,000-R2,000
  • Patent attorney: R5,000-R15,000
  • Annual renewal: R300-R800
  • Total first year: R6,500-R18,300
  • 20-year total: R15,000-R35,000+

Copyright Registration in South Africa

Registration is optional but recommended for enforcement purposes

  • Automatic Protection: Copyright exists from creation
  • Registration: Optional with Companies and Intellectual Property Commission (CIPC)
  • Duration: Life of author plus 50 years, or 50 years from publication for works
  • Cost: R200-R500 for registration
  • Benefits: Easier enforcement and legal presumption of ownership

⚖️ Enforcing Your IP Rights

What to do when someone infringes on your intellectual property

1. Document the Infringement

Gather evidence of the violation with dates, photos, and witness statements

2. Send Cease & Desist Letter

Formal notice demanding the infringer stop using your IP

3. Attempt Negotiation

Try to resolve the dispute through discussion or mediation

4. Consider Legal Action

If negotiation fails, consult attorneys about court action

5. Seek Injunction

Court order to immediately stop the infringement

6. Claim Damages

Seek compensation for losses and legal costs

Prevention is Best: Strong IP protection and monitoring reduce the need for enforcement actions. Regular IP audits help identify potential issues early.
