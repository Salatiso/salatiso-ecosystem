üöÄ Your Business Journey Starts Here

Complete these 10 mission cards to build your business foundation. Each card takes 10-30 minutes and creates proof for your LifeCV. Start with Card 1 and progress through them in order.

üìÑ Printing Instructions

For best results, print on A4 paper, double-sided. Each card is designed to fit on one side of A4. Cut along the edges for individual cards.

Business Name Brainstorm
Level 1 15 min
Objective: Create 3 potential business names that reflect your family's values and vision.
  1. Think about your family's heritage and values (Ubuntu, legacy, community)
  2. Brainstorm words related to your business idea
  3. Combine words to create memorable names
  4. Check if names are available (write them down)
  5. Pick your top 3 favorites
Proof Drop: Photo of your 3 business names + voice note explaining why each name matters to your family.
Tip: Include words like "Family", "Legacy", "Ubuntu", or "Community" to show your values.
Family Business Vision
Level 2 20 min
Objective: Define what success looks like for your family business in 5 years.
  1. Gather 2-3 family members for a discussion
  2. Ask: "What problems do we want to solve?"
  3. Discuss: "How will this business help our community?"
  4. Write down 3-5 vision statements
  5. Agree on your family's top priority
Proof Drop: Photo of your vision statements + family group photo with thumbs up.
Tip: Focus on impact, not just money. How will your business create shared prosperity?
CIPC Name Search
Level 2 10 min
Objective: Check if your preferred business names are available for registration.
  1. Go to cipc.co.za
  2. Find the "Name Search" or "Company Name Reservation" section
  3. Search for your top 3 business names
  4. Note which names are available
  5. If needed, modify names and search again
Proof Drop: Screenshot of CIPC search results showing at least one available name.
Tip: Names must end with "Proprietary Limited" or "(Pty) Ltd" for companies.
Document Preparation
Level 3 30 min
Objective: Gather and prepare all documents needed for company registration.
  1. Collect ID copies for all directors (parents/guardians)
  2. Get proof of residential addresses
  3. Prepare your chosen company name
  4. Decide on share structure (who owns what)
  5. Organize documents in a folder
Proof Drop: Photo of your organized document folder + checklist of completed items.
Tip: IDs need to be certified (copy from original). Police stations or banks can do this for R50-100.
Cost Planning
Level 2 15 min
Objective: Calculate startup costs and create a simple budget for your business.
  1. List all one-time startup costs
  2. Estimate monthly operating expenses
  3. Calculate your total first-year budget
  4. Identify potential funding sources
  5. Create a simple profit projection
Proof Drop: Photo of your budget spreadsheet + voice explanation of your numbers.
Tip: Start small! Many successful businesses began with under R5,000 investment.
Business Structure Decision
Level 3 20 min
Objective: Choose the right legal structure for your family business.
  1. Learn about Sole Proprietor vs Company
  2. Consider liability protection needs
  3. Think about tax implications
  4. Discuss with family members
  5. Make your recommendation
Proof Drop: Written explanation of your chosen structure + why it fits your family.
Tip: For most families, a (Pty) Ltd provides the best protection and credibility.
Market Research
Level 2 25 min
Objective: Understand who will buy your product/service and what they need.
  1. Identify your target customers
  2. Talk to 3 potential customers
  3. Ask about their problems and needs
  4. Find out what they currently pay for
  5. Note any common themes
Proof Drop: Summary of your customer interviews + key insights learned.
Tip: Start with people you know‚Äîfamily, friends, neighbors. Be genuine in your questions.
BizHelp Wizard Introduction
Level 1 10 min
Objective: Complete your first BizHelp Wizard session and get personalized guidance.
  1. Go to bizhelp-lifecv.web.app/wizard
  2. Create or sign in to your account
  3. Answer the personal assessment questions
  4. Review your customized recommendations
  5. Save your progress
Proof Drop: Screenshot of your Wizard completion + what surprised you most.
Tip: The Wizard learns from your answers‚Äîbe honest for the best recommendations!
Family Council Meeting
Level 2 30 min
Objective: Hold your first family business council to discuss and approve your plans.
  1. Set a meeting time with available family members
  2. Prepare an agenda (vision, names, structure)
  3. Present your research and recommendations
  4. Discuss concerns and get feedback
  5. Vote on key decisions (60% approval rule)
Proof Drop: Meeting minutes + group photo showing family consensus.
Tip: Keep it positive! Focus on shared benefits and how the business serves family values.
Registration Readiness Check
Level 3 15 min
Objective: Verify you have everything needed to register your company successfully.
  1. Check all documents are ready and certified
  2. Confirm company name is available
  3. Review share structure with family
  4. Prepare payment method for R1,250 fee
  5. Create submission checklist
Proof Drop: Completed checklist + photo of ready-to-submit document package.
Tip: Double-check everything! One missing document can delay registration by weeks.
