💰 Understanding Money in Business

Money is the fuel that keeps your business running. Understanding these basic concepts will help you make smart decisions and build a successful business that lasts.

💸 Money Flow: Income vs Expenses

Think of your business like a bucket. Money comes in (income) and money goes out (expenses). The goal is to have more money coming in than going out!

💵

Income (Money In)

What customers pay you for your products or services

🛒

Expenses (Money Out)

What you spend to run your business

💰

Profit (Money Left)

Income minus expenses = what you keep

R 0

📊 The Profit Formula

This is the most important equation in business. Memorize it!

Profit = Income - Expenses

Simple Example: You sell lemonade for R10 each. It costs R3 to make each glass.
Income: R10 | Expenses: R3 | Profit: R7 per glass
Sell 10 glasses = R70 income - R30 expenses = R37 profit!

📝 Types of Income & Expenses

💵 Income Examples

  • Product Sales: Selling physical items you make or buy
  • Service Fees: Money for work you do (tutoring, repairs, etc.)
  • Subscriptions: Monthly payments for ongoing services
  • Commissions: Percentage of sales from partnerships

🛒 Expense Examples

  • Materials: Raw materials, packaging, supplies
  • Tools & Equipment: Things you need to do your work
  • Marketing: Advertising, business cards, website
  • Transport: Getting to customers or delivering products

🎯 Money Management Tips

📊 Track Everything

Use a notebook or app to record every rand that comes in and goes out. This helps you see patterns and make better decisions.

💰 Save First, Spend Later

When you get paid, put some money aside for savings before you spend on fun things. Emergency savings protect your business.

📈 Price to Make Profit

Don't just cover your costs—price your products so you make a profit. Remember: Profit = Income - Expenses

🤝 Talk About Money

Don't be afraid to discuss prices with customers. Practice saying: "This costs R50 because..."
