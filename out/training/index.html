Sazi Life Academy · Family Training

Train the family, fuel the ecosystem.

The Salatiso Training Academy is where households, partners, and operators level up together. Inspired by PigeeBack’s proven blueprint, this curriculum turns daily missions into LifeCV evidence and readiness for every app inside Mlandeni-Notemba Investments.

Level up from personal readiness to ecosystem mentorship. Each tier unlocks new duties, platform access, and LifeCV proof channels that demonstrate mastery inside and outside the family business.

What Makes This Academy Different

Every lesson balances family care, professional readiness, and Ubuntu reciprocity. Learners always know why the mission matters, who benefits, and which app will capture the impact.

Family-First, All Ages

Every module is designed for multi-generational households. Parents, elders, teens, and kids learn together through story-led missions, action labs, and reflection prompts that plug straight into LifeCV.

  • Sazi story arcs introduce each concept with culturally rooted heroes.
  • Age-specific missions, printable cards, and accessibility-friendly narration.
  • Family councils certify completion and unlock shared rewards.

Ecosystem Mastery

The academy is the onboarding spine for the entire Salatiso ⇄ MNI ecosystem. Learners map how LifeSync, The Hub, PigeeBack, and BizHelp interlock and practise the handovers between layers.

  • System overviews rendered as interactive loops and mesh handoff drills.
  • Coaches simulate real incidents, budget reviews, and validator quorums.
  • Progressive unlocks show when to escalate from personal to household to professional duties.

LifeCV Proof Culture

Activity journals, voice notes, screenshots, and API logs all convert to LifeCV proof drops. Learners build portable evidence that validates their readiness for roles inside and beyond the family business.

  • Mission templates pre-tag proofs for reciprocity, safety, and governance.
  • Automated reminders to upload artefacts within 12 hours for bonus points.
  • Mentors co-sign submissions directly inside LifeCV for audit trails.

Progression Built for LifeCV Proof

The five-level ladder guides every family member, partner, and operator from personal readiness to region-wide mentorship. Each level syncs with LifeCV proof templates so achievements travel with the learner.

Understand the ecosystem, personal safety, and reciprocity basics.

Activities

  • Map your daily journeys and identify trust or safety touchpoints.
  • Identify your personal support circle and set up quick contact tiles.

Practice

  • Share your personal safety mantra with a buddy.

Belong · Join Your Household

Collaborate with family or housemates to keep everyone visible and safe.

Activities

  • Create or join your LifeSync household and assign visibility levels.
  • Design a rotating support schedule for chores, school runs, or deliveries.
  • Set shared alerts for check-ins, meds, or curfews using simple language.

Practice

  • Host a 10-minute household stand-up with a printed mission card.
  • Capture a photo of a shared resource with LifeCV tags.
