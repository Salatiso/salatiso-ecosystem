🏷️ Choosing Your Business Name

Your business name is your first impression. It should be memorable, meaningful, and legally available. This guide will help you find the perfect name for your business.

🧠 Name Brainstorming Workshop

Let's generate some name ideas! Use these techniques to spark creativity:

1. Word Association

Start with words related to your business idea and combine them:

Combinations: QuickFix Solutions, FixPro, Quick Solutions, etc.

2. Problem + Solution

Describe what problem you solve:

Example: "I help busy parents by delivering healthy meals" → HealthyEats Delivery

3. Personal Meaning

What makes your business special? Family values, location, unique approach?

Example: Family tradition of baking → Grandma's Kitchen, Ubuntu values → TogetherWeBuild

🏛️ CIPC Name Search & Registration

Before you get too attached to a name, check if it's available! South Africa's Companies and Intellectual Property Commission (CIPC) manages business name registrations.

1

Search Online

Visit CIPC website and use their name search tool. Check for exact matches and similar names.

2

Reserve Name

If available, you can reserve the name for 6 months while you prepare your registration.

3

Company Registration

When registering your company, the name becomes officially yours (for Pty Ltd companies).

4

Domain & Social

Check if the matching domain name and social media handles are available.

Visit CIPC Website

✅ Name Selection Checklist

Use this checklist to evaluate your name ideas:

📝 Spelling & Pronunciation

Easy to spell and pronounce? Will people remember how to say it?

🔍 Available Online

Domain name (.co.za) available? Social media handles free?

⚖️ Legal Check

Not already registered with CIPC? No trademark conflicts?

🎯 Meaningful

Does it tell people what you do? Reflect your values?

🌟 Memorable

Short, unique, and easy to remember? Stands out from competitors?

📈 Future Growth

Will it work if you expand to new products/services?

🎨 Basic Branding Concepts

Your brand is more than just a name—it's how people feel about your business. Start simple and build from there.

🎨

Logo Design

Your visual identity. Start with simple concepts—your initials, a symbol representing your business, or an illustration.

Tools: Canva (free), look for "logo maker"

🌈

Color Scheme

Choose 2-3 colors that represent your business. Consider what emotions they evoke.

Tip: Blue = trust, Green = growth, Orange = energy

✍️

Tagline

A short phrase that explains what makes you special. Keep it under 10 words.

Examples: "Quality you can trust", "Building futures together"

📱

Online Presence

Website, social media, business cards. Make sure they're consistent and professional.

Start with: Facebook page, Instagram, simple website
