What is the Salatiso Ecosystem?

The Salatiso Ecosystem is a comprehensive digital platform that includes 16 mobile apps and web services designed to help South African families, businesses, and communities thrive. Built by our family, for families everywhere, it integrates education, safety, financial management, business operations, and social justice into one unified ecosystem.
