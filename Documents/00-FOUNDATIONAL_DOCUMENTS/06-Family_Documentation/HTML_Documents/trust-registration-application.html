TRUST DEED AND PURPOSE

1. TRUST PURPOSE AND OBJECTIVES

Primary Purpose:

To hold, manage, and preserve family wealth for the benefit of the Mdeni family lineage, ensuring the perpetuation of Ubuntu values, cultural heritage, and economic prosperity across generations.

Specific Objectives:

  • Preserve and grow family wealth through prudent investment and business ownership
  • Provide education and development opportunities for all beneficiaries
  • Maintain family unity and Ubuntu principles in all trust activities
  • Support community development and social responsibility initiatives
  • Ensure sustainable succession planning for future generations
  • Protect family assets from external risks and potential claims
  • Honor the memory and values of Mlandeli and Notemba Mdeni
