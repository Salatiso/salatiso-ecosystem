8.1 Board Composition and Powers

Minimum Directors: 2 (two) directors required at all times

Board Meetings: Quarterly meetings required, with additional meetings as needed

Quorum: Majority of directors present and voting

Chairman: Elected by board, with casting vote in case of ties

8.2 Shareholder Rights and Restrictions

Family-Only Ownership Restriction

Shares may only be held by direct descendants of Mlandeli Nelson Mdeni and Nozukile Cynthia Mdeni. No shares may be transferred to non-family members under any circumstances.
